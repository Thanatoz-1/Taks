Preparing the dataset¶

Here we are going to prepare the dataset for the intent classifier training that we are going to use to train our rasa classifier and detect the consignee and the shipper as well as the forwarder.

What are we doing in this notebook?¶

The following process we are following here in this notebook to achieve the solution:

  • converting the PDfs to images
  • Thresholding images to remove any useless artifacts
  • Use the thresholded image in tesseract ocr to detect text

After we are done with extracting text using the OCR, we are going to train our Intent Classifier to train on a few templates to easily identify text. I have used 25 templates from the HAWB dataset to train the classifier for extracting 3 entities: Forwarding_comapany_name, Shipper_company_name, & Consignee_company_name.
